The Great Pyramid of Giza (also known as the Pyramid of Khufu or the Pyramid of
Cheops) is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza Necropolis
bordering what is now El Giza, Egypt. It is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient
World, and the only one to remain largely intact.
